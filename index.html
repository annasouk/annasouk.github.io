About

Research & Background

I develop tactile sensing systems and robot control software for the GenTact project, building skin-like sensors that give robots a sense of touch. My work spans URDF modeling, ROS 2 integration, point cloud visualization, and real-time teleoperation.

I also have experience with autonomous racing platforms and numerical methods for engineering applications.
